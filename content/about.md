# About This Knowledge Base
This site is a personal knowledge base on PC and console video games, created as the final project for a course on information organization and the discipline of organizing.

## Purpose
The goal was to move beyond a simple collection of notes and build a functional **organizing system** - one that supports searching for known items, browsing through clear categories, and discovering unexpected connections through intentional internal links.

## Structure
The knowledge base is organized into six primary categories:

- Game Genres
- Platforms & Hardware
- Gameplay Mechanics & Systems (the most developed section)
- Game Development & Design
- History & Evolution
- Community, Modding & Culture

Gameplay Mechanics & Systems received the deepest treatment because systems are the foundation that genres, platforms, history, and community all interact with.
